What is Midsouth Fight Club?



Midsouth Fight Club is a Street Fighter 6 League comprised of teams spanning the Midsouthern states of the USA. Teams consist of 8 players per team with a maximum of two teams per state (currently). Teams are divided into a introductory 3v3 card, and a main 5v5 card. Matches between teams are played and streamed every other Sunday!
